// 01 · ABOUT THE TEAM

Who We Are

We are Bot Busters — the first FTC team in Macedonia. Though relatively new to the competition scene, we have already made our mark on the international stage, competing at the FTC Regional in Thessaloniki, Greece, where we secured our first victory against teams with years of experience.


This season we also took part in the FTC Kickathon by Quantum Robotics in Romania — an event where participants design and build a complete robot in under 24 hours. A challenge that put our teamwork, technical skills, and dedication to the ultimate test. Now in our second year, we continue pushing the limits of what a young team from the Balkans can achieve.
